What is Melodity?

Melodity - DIY Musician blog was launched by Keyla Jones, editor-in-chief, in April of 2021.

Melodity is made for musicians of all skill levels to help them get gigs, make money from their music, record songs at home, write original songs, and much more. These topics come from real-life experience with tips and tricks every musician should know, but rarely gets told.
The hope is to inspire, educate, and mentor all the aspiring musicians out there to achieve their dreams one post at a time.

“I started out singing in my closet to paid music festivals. I learned a lot along the way later on, and I just wish someone had told me these tricks earlier on, instead of learning the hard way.” ~Keyla Jones



Keyla Jones, Founder and Editor-in-Chief

Keyla Jones is a professional singer-songwriter with experience performing all over the United States in county fairs, music festivals, and other venues.

HOW TO FIND LOCAL OPEN MICS

FIND YOUR UNIQUE EDGE
