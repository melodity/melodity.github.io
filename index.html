About Us

WHAT IS MELODITY?

Melodity is made for musicians of all skill levels to help them get gigs, make money from their music, record songs at home, write original songs, and much more. These topics come from real-life experience with tips and tricks every musician should know, but rarely gets told.
The hope is to inspire, educate, and mentor all the aspiring musicians out there to achieve their dreams one post at a time.

How to Find Local Open Mics

HOW TO FIND LOCAL OPEN MICS

It is not easy to get up in front of a crowd and sing, but this how-to guide is going to provide you with everything you need to help you be a success.
From finding the right type of open mic for your genre and skill level to giving you all the pro tips you might not have thought of.
After all, no one wants to be a little teen popstar singing a love song in a biker bar.

How to Find Your Unique Edge

FIND YOUR UNIQUE EDGE

Do your covers sound like every other musician? Does it feel like something is just missing, like the spark in your cover isn’t lighting up? Maybe it’s time to try and find your unique edge.
This guide is going to show you how to experiment with different genres and styles of music/singing to find your sweet spot, then incorporate them into your songs.
